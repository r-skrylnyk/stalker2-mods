# stalker2-mods

A collection of restored and original mods for S.T.A.L.K.E.R. 2: Heart of Chornobyl.
Includes both revived unsupported mods and original work.

**Tested on:** Patch 1.8 / 1.9

## Mods

| # | Mod | Patch | Notes |
|---|-----|-------|-------|
| 1 | [Fraction Radio](FractionRadio_with_ue4ss/readme.md) | 1.8+ | Portable in-game radio with station hotkeys — requires UE4SS |
| 2 | [Sound Weapons from SoC](Sound%20weapons%20from%20SoC/readme.md) | 1.8+ | Authentic weapon sounds from Shadow of Chernobyl |
| 3 | [Weird Water Undrunked](Weird%20Water%20Undrunked/readme.md) | 1.8+ | Removes drunkenness effect from the Weird Water artifact |
| 4 | [GP37V2 Customizable](GP37V2_Customizable/) | 1.9 | Adds scope/attachment customization to GP37V2 (bpatch) |
| 5 | [Unique Weapons — Removable Scopes and Silencers](Unique%20Weapons%20-%20Removable%20Scopes%20and%20Silencers/readme.md) | 1.9+ | Makes preinstalled scopes/silencers removable on unique weapons |
