# Sound weapons from S.T.A.L.K.E.R. Shadow of Chernobyl.
Replaces the default rifle sounds with authentic audio from the original S.T.A.L.K.E.R.: Shadow of Chernobyl, bringing a nostalgic feel to one of the Zone's most iconic weapons.

Features:
Replaced single shot and automatic fire sounds
Works correctly both indoors and outdoors

Compatibility:
Tested on Patch 1.8

Should not conflict with other mods unless they modify weapon audio files
Installation: Place .pak file into Stalker2/Content/Paks/~mods/ folder.
